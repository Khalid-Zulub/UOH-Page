Powers, Duties & Responsibilities of the University Of Hargeisa Senate:

  • Examine and approve the academic calendar of the University
  • Approve the University's various academic programs
  • Approve criteria for admission and enrollment of students
  • Oversee quality assurance and excellence of programs
  • Set criteria for the determination of academic standards
  • Determine criteria for graduation and its ceremony processions
  • approve tuition fees to be charged by the University
  • Formulate policies and guidelines for planning and utilization of resources
  • Formulate policy and devise mechanisms for staff requirement, retention and promotion
  • Determine its own rules and procedures
