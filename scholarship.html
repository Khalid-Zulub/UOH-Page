Student Registration & Scholarship

University of Hargeisa - Admissions Section

Student Registration Overview

Registration allows students to officially become part of the University of Hargeisa community. It is an important step before applying for scholarships and ensures accurate academic records.

  • Fill out your registration form and submit all required documents.
  • Meet academic requirements for scholarship eligibility.
  • Update your contact and program details every semester.
  • Participate in university orientation and advising sessions.

Scholarship Opportunities

The University of Hargeisa offers a variety of scholarships to support students with academic excellence and financial need. Scholarships help reduce tuition costs and encourage students to achieve their educational goals.

  • Merit-based scholarships for top-performing students.
  • Need-based scholarships for students from low-income families.
  • Special partnership scholarships funded by organizations or donors.
  • Renewable scholarships for continuing academic success.

How to Apply

Students can apply for scholarships during registration by completing the scholarship form and submitting supporting documents such as academic transcripts and recommendation letters.

The University of Hargeisa is committed to supporting talented and hardworking students through fair and transparent scholarship programs.
