Welcome to University of Hargeisa

The University of Hargeisa is public higher educational institution located in Hargeisa Somaliland. The University was established in 2000 with only two faculties. Currently, it consists of 10 Colleges with more than 30 undergraduate programs. It has a school of Graduate Studies, which now provides more than ten postgraduate programs. The University has two campuses that occupy 75,000m2 in total. Despite its short existence, the University has succeeded in becoming the biggest and leading University in Somaliland.
