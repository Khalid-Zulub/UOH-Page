About Us

Introduction

The University of Hargeisa, the largest higher education institution in Somaliland, was established to drive national development through research and education. Initially focused on teaching, the university expanded its mission in 2016 to include research and community service, aiming to address local, national, and global challenges.

Vision

To be a leading university renowned for fostering high-quality, impactful research and innovation that addresses local, regional, and global challenges, while contributing to the advancement of knowledge and sustainable development across various disciplines.

Mission

The University of Hargeisa aims to promote research excellence and innovation through capacity building, strategic partnerships, and cutting-edge research facilities. By supporting faculty, students, and community stakeholders, UoH seeks to enhance knowledge transfer, address societal challenges, and contribute to sustainable development through impactful research and innovation.

“Our commitment is to advance knowledge and bridge the gap between academia and community, driving progress through impactful research and meaningful service.”

Khalid Ahmed Abdi, Director, Research, Publications and Community Services
