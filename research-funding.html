Research Funding

The University of Hargeisa offers research funding to support innovation, problem-solving, and academic excellence across various disciplines.

Funding Opportunities

  • Internal University Research Grants for staff and postgraduate students.
  • Partnerships with international donors and development agencies.
  • Grants for community-based and applied research projects.
  • Funding for conferences, journal publications, and fieldwork.

Objectives of Research Funding

The funding program encourages innovation, collaboration, and impact-oriented research. It helps researchers transform their ideas into solutions that address real challenges in Somaliland — such as health, education, peacebuilding, and environmental sustainability.
