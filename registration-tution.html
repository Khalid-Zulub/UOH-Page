Student Registration & Tutition

University of Hargeisa - Admissions Section

About Student Registration

The University of Hargeisa ensures that all students are properly registered at the beginning of each academic year or semester. Registration confirms a student's place in the university and allows access to academic and administrative services.

  • Complete the online or in-person registration form.
  • Submit required documents, including ID, transcripts, and admission letter.
  • Confirm course selections with your faculty advisor.
  • Receive your official student ID and registration number.

About Tuition Fees

Tuition fees help the University of Hargeisa provide quality education, facilities, and academic resources. Students are required to pay their fees before the start of each semester to maintain active enrollment.

  • Tuition varies by faculty and program.
  • Payments can be made via bank deposit or online payment portals.
  • Deadlines for fee payments are announced at the start of every semester.
  • Refunds follow university policy in case of withdrawal or deferment.

"Our goal is to make registration and tuition payment smooth, transparent, and student-friendly".
